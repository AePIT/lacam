What we offer you

Lacam has a successful track record and prides itself to offer a wide variety of quality, cost efficient industrial and commercial contract cleaning services strictly in adherence to industry specifications and procedures. We do offer services from daily industrial and commercial cleaning to large corporates to smaller businesses requiring less frequent cleaning.

Our History

Lacam Cleaning Services (Pty) Ltd is an industrial and commercial Contract Cleaning Company based in the Vaal Triangle Gauteng. The company was established in July 2014 and successfully provides industrial and commercial contract cleaning services to 7 major engineering and structural steel fabricators in the Vaal Triangle. Our track record speaks for itself. Lacam prides itself to be 100% woman owned and is a 70% black owned entity. Lacam is a level 2 BBBEE certified company and to date, the company has created over 40 jobs and is operational in Meyerton, Vereeniging, Vanderbijlpark and Sasolburg.
